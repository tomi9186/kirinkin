UNTOUCHED PARADISE

Vis: Croatia's Last Authentic Island

Closed to tourists until 1989 as a military zone, Vis remains Croatia's best-kept secret. Crystal-clear waters, hidden coves like Stiniva (Europe's best beach 2016), and vineyards untouched by mass tourism await just beyond your door.

Read More

RICH HERITAGE

Ancient History, Modern Tranquility

Home to Europe's oldest Greek colony (Issa, 4th century BC), British naval tunnels from WWII, and Tito's cave hideout. Hike Hum hill for panoramic views, dive Blue Cave, or taste rare Vugava wines from family estates – authentic island life preserved.

EXPERIENCE VIS

Discover the island of Vis

Kut Old Quarter

Stroll through narrow stone streets, traditional townhouses and quiet waterfront promenades in the most authentic part of Vis. Kut offers a timeless atmosphere just steps from Palazzo Kirinkin.

Learn More

Crystal-Clear Beaches

Swim in turquoise bays, sunbathe on pebble coves and explore hidden swimming spots reachable by boat. Vis is home to some of Croatia’s most pristine and peaceful beaches.

Learn More

Culture & Gastronomy

Taste fresh seafood, local wines and traditional Dalmatian recipes in charming taverns and seaside restaurants. Discover Vis through its stories, festivals and slow island lifestyle.

Learn More

Island Experience

Sail to nearby islets, visit the famous Blue Cave, or join a wine tour through the island’s vineyards. Create your own tailor-made Vis adventure from the comfort of Palazzo Kirinkin.
