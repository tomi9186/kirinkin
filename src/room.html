Heritage Grand room two

from 210 EUR / per night

Enjoy a spacious 45 m² one-bedroom apartment with a fully equipped kitchen, private patio, and all modern amenities for a comfortable stay.

About Accommodation

Relax in this spacious 45 m² one-bedroom apartment designed to provide comfort and convenience during your stay. The apartment features a separate bedroom with a queen bed and a living room with a sofa bed, making it ideal for couples, families, or small groups.

Guests can enjoy a fully equipped private kitchen including a stovetop, oven, refrigerator, microwave, dishwasher, coffee machine, electric kettle, kitchenware, and a dining table. The apartment also includes a washing machine, making it perfect for longer stays.

The modern bathroom features a walk-in shower, fresh towels, and a hairdryer. Additional amenities include air conditioning, flat-screen TV, free Wi-Fi, and a private patio with outdoor furniture where guests can relax. The apartment also offers a private entrance and a comfortable seating area.

Why Choose Apartment Kirinkin?

Comfort, Location & Authentic
Island Experience in Vis

Comfortable Apartment

Enjoy a spacious one-bedroom apartment with a separate living room, air-conditioning, a washing machine, and a fully equipped kitchen designed for a relaxing and independent stay.

Excellent Location

Located just a 5-minute walk from Beach Vagan and close to Srebrna Bay, the apartment offers easy access to some of the most beautiful beaches and coastal views on the island of Vis.

Free Wi-Fi & Modern Amenities

Stay connected with free high-speed Wi-Fi while enjoying modern comforts including air conditioning, a fully equipped kitchen, outdoor seating, and entertainment options.

Leisure Activities

Guests can enjoy nearby activities such as tennis, fishing, hiking, cycling, and guided walking tours – perfect for exploring the natural beauty of Vis.

Shuttle Service

A convenient paid shuttle service is available for guests, making transfers to nearby locations and transport hubs easy and stress-free.

Loved by Couples

Couples particularly love the location and atmosphere of the apartment, giving it an excellent rating of 9.7 for a two-person trip.
